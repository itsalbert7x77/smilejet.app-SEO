Why Hanoi for Dental Implants?

Vietnam's capital has emerged as a serious contender for dental tourism in Southeast Asia. Hanoi offers a unique combination that few cities can match: internationally trained dentists, clinics equipped with the same technology and implant brands used in Sydney or London, and prices that are a fraction of what you'd pay at home.

Unlike Bangkok or Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi has a more relaxed pace — ideal for recovery. The city's Old Quarter, lakeside cafes, and world-renowned street food scene make for a far more enjoyable post-surgery experience than your local dental office waiting room. With direct flights from major cities across Asia, Australia, and Europe, getting here is easy.

But not all clinics are equal. That's why SmileJet pre-screens and verifies clinics based on dentist qualifications, facility standards, and real patient feedback. Below are the top 9 verified dental implant clinics in Hanoi, ranked by experience, reviews, and overall quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do dental implants cost in Hanoi?

Dental implants in Hanoi cost between $400 and $1,500 per implant depending on the brand and clinic. Korean implants (Osstem, Dentium) start at $400–$800, while premium Swiss and Swedish brands (Straumann, Nobel Biocare) range from $900–$1,500. All-on-4 full arch restorations start from around $5,000–$8,500. This represents savings of 60–80% compared to Australia, the US, or the UK.

Which is the best dental implant clinic in Hanoi?

The best dental implant clinic in Hanoi depends on your priorities. Picasso Dental Clinic leads with 70,000+ patients from 62 countries and a 5.0 rating. Greenfield Dental Clinic has the highest review volume with 51 verified reviews and a 4.8 rating. Westcoast International is Vietnam's longest-established foreign-invested dental clinic (since 2004). Australian Dental Clinic has served international patients since 2006. Use SmileJet to compare all verified clinics side by side.

Is it safe to get dental implants in Hanoi?

Yes, dental implants in Hanoi are safe when performed at reputable clinics. The clinics listed on SmileJet are pre-screened for quality and safety standards. Many Hanoi clinics use the same implant brands (Nobel Biocare, Straumann, Osstem) as top Western practices and employ internationally trained, English-speaking dentists. Look for clinics with verified patient reviews, modern technology like 3D CBCT scanning, and transparent pricing.

What implant brands are available at Hanoi dental clinics?

Hanoi dental clinics offer a wide range of implant brands. Premium options include Straumann (Switzerland), Nobel Biocare (Sweden/USA), and BioHorizons (USA). Value options include Osstem and Dentium (South Korea), Tekka (France), and NeoDent (Straumann Group). The brand you choose affects both price and warranty. SmileJet-verified clinics use genuine implants with full manufacturer traceability.

How long do I need to stay in Hanoi for dental implants?

A standard dental implant requires two trips to Hanoi. The first trip (5–7 days) covers consultation, 3D scanning, implant placement, and initial recovery. The second trip (3–5 days), typically 3–6 months later, is for fitting the permanent crown. Some Hanoi clinics offer same-day implant protocols where temporary crowns are placed immediately. Hanoi is an excellent city to recover in, with world-class food, cultural attractions, and affordable hotels.
